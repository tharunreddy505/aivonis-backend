3. Why This Pricing Works

  • High Margin Safety Net: Your base cost is very low (~$0.02/min). Charging $0.10-$0.25/min gives you a massive buffer for fluctuating AI API costs or heavy usage.
  • Breakage Model: In the monthly plans ($99), most users won't use the full 1,000 minutes. This increases your actual profit margin significantly beyond the estimates above.
  • Competitor Comparison: Fonio.ai charges €0.20/min (~$0.22). By matching or slightly undercutting this (or offering better features like included recordings), you remain competitive.
