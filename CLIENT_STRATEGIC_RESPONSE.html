Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a market-driven SaaS pricing strategy for AIvonis. Analysis of competitors (Air.ai, Bland.ai, Fonio.ai) reveals a gap for a mid-market, self-service solution that avoids high upfront licensing fees (~$25k) while offering better "all-in-one" value than raw infrastructure providers like Vapi.

Recommendation: A 3-tier subscription model reducing friction for entry ($49/mo) while securing healthy gross margins (55%+) on usage.
