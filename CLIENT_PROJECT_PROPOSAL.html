Fonio: Technical & Commercial Proposal

This document outlines the technical infrastructure, operational costs, and recommended commercial pricing strategy for the Fonio AI Voice Platform.

1. System Architecture & Resources

The platform is built on a modern, scalable stack leveraging best-in-class AI and Telephony providers.

Core Intelligence (The "Brain")

  • Provider: OpenAI
  • Model: gpt-4o-mini
  • Function: Understands user intent, manages conversation flow, and generates intelligent responses in real-time.
  • Reason for Choice: Offers the best balance of speed (low latency for voice) and cost-efficiency.

Telephony Infrastructure (The "Phone Line")

  • Provider: Twilio
  • Function: Manages global phone numbers, inbound/outbound call routing, and reliable connection to carrier networks.
  • Capabilities: Support for local and toll-free numbers in 100+ countries.

Voice Synthesis (The "Speaker")

  • Provider: Amazon Polly (via Twilio)
  • Model: Neural TTS
  • Function: Converts AI text into lifelike, natural-sounding human speech.

Email Delivery (Post-Call)

  • Protocol: SMTP (via Nodemailer)
  • Function: Instantly sends call summaries and transcripts to the user after every call.

Hosting & Application

  • Frontend: Next.js 16 (React 19)
  • Database: SQLite (Dev) / Postgres (Production Ready)
  • Security: NextAuth.js for secure session management.

3. Recommended Client Pricing Strategy

To ensure profitability and sustainability, we recommend the following pricing tiers for your end customers.

Starter (Pay-As-You-Go)

Best for sporadic usage or testing.

$0.25 / minute
  • No monthly commitment
  • Full access to all AI agents
  • Phone number: $5/month

Margin: ~90% (Profit: $0.23/min)

Business Pro (Subscription)

For businesses with regular call volume.

$99 / month
  • 1,000 Minutes included
  • 1 Dedicated Phone Number included
  • Call Recordings included
  • Priority Support

Margin: ~75% (Profit: ~$75/user)

Strategic Note: This pricing model ensures that even your heavy users are profitable, while the subscription model provides predictable recurring revenue. The "Business Pro" plan is priced competitively against market leaders like Fonio.ai while offering superior margins.
